Privacy Policy for Omnipate

Effective Date: 20.09.2024

Introduction

Welcome to Omnipate. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application. By using Omnipate, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

1. Audio Data

If you choose to do so, Omnipate records audio data and sends it to our backend servers hosted on Google Cloud. The recorded audio is processed using OpenAI's Whisper model to convert it into text. Please note that the audio data is not stored by us, and OpenAI's privacy policies apply to the handling of your data during processing. We do not retain any of the audio or text generated by this service.

2. LinkedIn OAuth Tokens

When you log in to Omnipate using your LinkedIn account, we collect and store your OAuth tokens to authenticate your access to LinkedIn. These tokens are securely stored and are only accessible by you. No one else, including Omnipate administrators or other users, can access your LinkedIn token or any of your LinkedIn user information.

Data Usage

The information we collect is used solely for providing the services you request. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties except as required to process the data as described above (e.g., using OpenAI's Whisper model for audio processing).

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. OAuth tokens and any other sensitive information are encrypted and stored in secure systems. Only you have access to your LinkedIn data.

Third-Party Services

Our application uses third-party services like OpenAI and Google Cloud. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for further information:

  • Google Cloud Privacy Policy
