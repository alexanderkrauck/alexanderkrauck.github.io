How to Delete Your Account and Data

To delete your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Log into the app using your account credentials.
  2. Go to your profile settings by pressing the button in the top-right corner of the screen.
  3. Select the option labeled "Delete Account and All Data".
  4. Confirm the deletion by following the on-screen prompts.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers and the local mobile app installation. This action is irreversible and the data will not be recoverable.
